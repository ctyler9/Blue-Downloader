  • About

    Blue Downloader is a revolutionary new way to download files from the internet and also surf the web simpler than ever before. Down below Blue Downloader supplies links for you to download all of your favorite files, whether a document from dropbox.com, or a PDF from box.com, Blue Downloader helps you download what you need at your own convienience, from multiple sites, all in one app. If you don't need to download anything, the web browser on the bottom left can be used to surf the web, simply without any distractions.

    Instructions

    1. Press the plus sign in the lower left hand corner to open the browser

    2. Select the website you want to access from the home screen

    3. Click on the file you want, and let Blue Downloader do the rest
